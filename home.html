About Us

A story marking the lives of children

This project was brought to life because the families with young unschooled children in this part of Namwera, Malawi, needed a school which is within walking distance. This is a very poor under-served area of Malawi. There are no schools nearby for young children and where there is a primary school, many little girls are not encouraged or given the opportunity to attend.
