The School

The Gopie Community School is the heart of the Namwera Project. Fostering and supporting the desire to learn in young children has always been a tenet at the core of the Project. It's a school within walking distance, a school that teaches whoever wishes to learn, and a school that has become a beacon within the community. To grant all an equal opportunity, and to give future generations a better chance at success. These are the hopes that the Gopie Community School laid its foundations upon.
