Donate Here

Donating is the best way to support the cause we at the Namwera Project are working towards. When you donate, the work we do becomes that much easier. We're working towards creating a self-sustained community in Malosa, and every donation goes towards that goal. Whether that be paying the teacher working at the Gopie Community School, or buying supplies for the skills centre, or helping install solar panels. Your support now helps the community support itself in the future.

Namwera Project's Relief Fund

As of now, The Namwera Project is focusing all of its efforts on this one community here in Namwera. Your donation goes directly towards supporting the people of this village, towards supporting thechildren who wish to go to the Gopie Community School, and to the continued progress of a determined community.
